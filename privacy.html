Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

Rally Chrono ("the app") is developed by Angelo Sasso. This page explains, in plain terms, how the app handles your information.

Data collection

The app does not collect, store or transmit any personal data. All sessions, settings and preferences you create are stored locally on your device, in the iOS sandbox that belongs to Rally Chrono.

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDK. It does not perform any network call to remote servers operated by the developer.

System diagnostics

The app uses Apple's MetricKit framework to receive anonymous performance and energy diagnostics from iOS. This data is delivered by the operating system in aggregated form, never leaves your device through the app, and is used only to detect and fix issues during development.

Permissions

Rally Chrono does not request access to location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, health data or any other sensitive system permission.

Data sharing

When you choose to export one or more sessions as a text report, the file is generated on your device and shared through the standard iOS share sheet (Mail, AirDrop, Messages, etc.). The destination of that share is entirely chosen by you.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. It does not target children specifically and does not collect any personal data, including from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page with a new "last updated" date. Material changes will also be highlighted in the app release notes.

Contact

Rally Chrono ("the app") is developed by Angelo Sasso.
