Frequently asked questions

Does Rally Chrono need an internet connection?

No. The app works fully offline. It never connects to remote servers.

Where are my sessions stored?

Only on your device. Sessions are never uploaded anywhere by the app.

Can I export my sessions?

Yes. Open the sessions list (top-left icon on the main screen), tap the menu and choose Export all or Select to export. The export is a plain-text report that you can share through any iOS-supported channel (Mail, AirDrop, Messages, Notes…).

How do I configure the sectors?

Tap the slider icon at the top right of the main screen. From there you can add, remove and edit the target time of each sector. Sectors are saved automatically.

Why doesn't my screen turn off during a run?

It's intentional. In a regularity run you must always see the current split, so Rally Chrono prevents your iPhone from auto-locking while a run is active. The screen returns to normal behaviour as soon as you reset the run.

Does the app support landscape mode?

Rally Chrono is designed for portrait, one-handed use. This keeps the layout consistent for co-drivers strapped in the navigator seat.

Which languages does the app support?

Italian, English, French, Spanish, Russian and Simplified Chinese. The app follows your iPhone's language automatically.

Will there be an iPad / Apple Watch version?

Not at the moment. Rally Chrono is currently iPhone-only. Feature requests are always welcome at the address above.
