The stopwatch built for the stage.

Rally Chrono is the regularity rally stopwatch for co-drivers — sectors, color-coded splits and a session archive, always in your pocket.

Built for regularity.

Everything a co-driver needs during a stage, nothing they don't.

Large, glanceable timer

A timer you can read with one eye on the road book — optimised for Dynamic Type and never dims your screen.

Custom sectors

Set the number of sectors and target times that match your road book. Edit them in seconds.

Color-coded splits

Green on target, orange for warning, red when you're off — readable at a glance.

Session archive

Save every run, rename, browse and share later — your race history is always one tap away.

Plain-text export

Export single sessions or your full archive as a tidy text report. Share via Mail, AirDrop or Messages.

Private by design

No account, no ads, no analytics SDK. Everything stays on your iPhone.
